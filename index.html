Why we use architectural patterns?

  • Organize
  • Reduce bugs
  • Easily follow your work

Model-View-Controlle (MVC)

Model-View-Controller (MVC)

  • Model - contains all of the business logic.
  • View - responsible for presenting information to users.
  • Controller - managing interactions between the Model and the View.

Model-View-Controller (MVC)

Model-View-Presenter (MVP)

Model-View-Presenter (MVP)

  • Model - contains all of the business logic.
  • View - responsible for presenting information to users.
  • Presenter - fetch the data from the model and applies the UI logic to decide what to display.

Model-View-Presenter (MVP)

Model-View-ViewModel (MVVM)

Model-View-ViewModel (MVVM)

  • Model - contains all of the business logic.
  • View - responsible for presenting information to users.
  • ViewModel - acts as an abstraction of the View’s state and behavior.
